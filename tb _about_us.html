Top bangers is an Brittish video-sharing website headquartered in The Ritz, london. former PayPal employee— Sam Doherty— created the service in February 2006. Google bought the site in November 2012 for GBP£7.25 billion; Top bangers now operates as one of Google's subsidiaries.
